About Elysium

This devlog documents the development of Elysium: The Forsaken Dungeons. Follow along as we share progress, challenges, and updates! And as a reminder, this is an indie project handled by three college students, so things are subject to change!

Phases:

  • Phase 1: Primarily includes the implementation of basic game mechanics, back-end, server mechanics, and dungeon/loot generation.
  • Phase 2: Game is further expanded through design and concept art. This phase will introduce various things such as enemy design, card design, dungeon design, and much more.
  • Phase 3.1: Finishing touches are put onto the game, and cleanup functions are implemented to ensure there are no faults in the game's mechanics.
  • Phase 3.2: The game is completed enough for showcase purposes and advertisement. Future plans include having the game presented at conferences like RDC25 and more.
